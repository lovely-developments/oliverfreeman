Lovely Drinks

  • wireframes
  • design
  • front-end
  • 18 months

Before the start of the project, WWF-Australia had a website that was reaching it’s seventh birthday. It wasn’t responsive, and the design had also started to show it’s age.
There were three simultaneous projects to coincide with WWF-Australia’s new five year strategy– redesign the website, completely re-work the content, and find a new CMS to keep up with the content teams daily updates.

Wireframes

Wireframes were exceptionally important to get right at the beginning of this project as WWF had an immense wealth of information to showcase to users. We had to concentrate on a way to clean up and modernise the layout, but not lose the large amount of features on each page that had grown and evolved over the years, such as resource documents, maps, forms and videos.
